About the Show

Arcane is an animated series set in the universe of the popular game League of Legends. It premeired on Netflix in 2021, the show follows the complex relationship between two sisters, Vi and Jinx, as they navigate a world filled with magic, technology, and political tensions in the cities of Piltover and Zaun.

Blending stunning visuals with deep storytelling, Arcane explores themes of family, identity, and power, resonating with audiences worldwide. The show received critical acclaim for its art style, character depth, and emotional narrative, making it a groundbreaking addition to both animation and video game adaptations.

Fun Facts

  • Did you know? Arcane's unique animation style is a blend of 2D and 3D techniques by French studio Fortiche Productions.
  • The series took over six years to create, reflecting the dedication to high-quality storytelling and visuals.
  • Arcane is the first Netflix show based on a video game to achieve widespread critical acclaim, even winning an Emmy Award for Outstanding Animated Program.
